store.cw.vu

This website demonstrates vishual hierarchy. Looking at the design structure visual characteristics - for example, the menu and categories contents and arangement elements presented is organized into related sections or categories showing their order of importance so that customers can understand the informations easily, impress by the informations presented, influence customers perceptions, and lead customers to desired actions.

Contrast

The Bank of the South Pacific Vanuatu

bsp.com.vu

I think the Bank of South Pacific Vanuatu Limited page displays the great use of contrast. In a website color contrast is so important because the text and background colors can determine how people can interact with the page and its content that is written in those colors. It helps you organize your design, displays which parts of your design are most important and signal viewers to focus on those. Good color contrast means all users can see your content no matter the device-width they,re using or the lighting of their surroundings.
